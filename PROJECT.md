# PROJECT.md — RAW Network
> Always loaded first. This is the north star.

## Project Name

**RAW Network**

## Primary Objective

An autonomous content intelligence system that transforms any raw input — voice, text, photo, video — into platform-ready, QJ RAW-voiced content distributed across all channels with a single approval tap.

## Vision

RAW Network exists because humans are disappearing in the age of AI. This system doesn't replace the human voice — it *amplifies* it. It takes the raw, unfiltered signal of who you are and makes it visible everywhere, on every platform, without losing authenticity.

**Core belief:** Humans are not datasets. They are dynamic systems of choices, beliefs, traumas, victories, and contradictions. RAW Network captures that signal and distributes it at scale.

## Who It's For

- Veterans rebuilding after combat who have something to say
- Creators fighting algorithmic invisibility
- Solo founders who can't afford a content team but refuse to be silent
- Anyone who feels the gap between who they are and who the world thinks they are

## System Architecture (high-level)

```
INPUT (voice/text/photo/video)
    ↓
INGEST ENGINE (normalize, transcribe, extract signal)
    ↓
VOICE ENGINE (QJ RAW tone, style, identity enforcement)
    ↓
CONTENT ENGINE (format for each platform — X, IG, LinkedIn, YouTube, TikTok, blog, email)
    ↓
APPROVAL GATE (single tap: approve / edit / reject)
    ↓
DISTRIBUTION ENGINE (n8n workflows → deploy to all channels)
    ↓
FEEDBACK LOOP (analytics, engagement tracking, pattern recognition)
```

## Core Components

1. **Ingest Engine** — Accepts any raw input format, normalizes to structured data
2. **Voice Engine** — QJ RAW identity layer — tone, vocabulary, cadence, authenticity enforcement
3. **Content Engine** — Platform-aware formatting (character limits, aspect ratios, hashtag strategy)
4. **Approval Interface** — Single-tap review with edit capability (UIUX Promax constraints)
5. **Distribution Engine** — n8n workflow automation for multi-channel deployment
6. **Feedback Loop** — Analytics ingestion, engagement tracking, content evolution
