# RAW Framework

The foundational architecture for cognitive mapping and human digital twin modeling.

## Core Principles

**1. Pattern Recognition**
Human behavior is not random. It follows patterns — decision trees, trauma responses, identity markers. RAW identifies and maps these patterns.

**2. Reflective Feedback**
You cannot change what you cannot see. The framework creates mirrors. Real-time synchronicity journaling. Pattern recognition without judgment.

**3. Identity as System**
You are not a fixed thing. You are a dynamic system of beliefs, decisions, habits, and evolution. RAW models this as a living architecture.

**4. The 40.03XG Model**
Personal time decelerates while the world accelerates. The framework tracks the collision point — where your internal reality meets external reality. That's where transformation happens.

## How It Works

- **Cognitive Mapping:** Spatial visualization of thought patterns and decision nodes
- **Behavioral Tracking:** Real-time logging of choices, responses, and pattern emergence
- **Synchronicity Journal:** Pattern recognition across seemingly unrelated events
- **Reflective Interface:** Feedback loops that show you what you're actually doing (not what you think you're doing)

## The Output

A human digital twin. A working model of how you think. A map of who you actually are — not who you pretend to be.

## Use Cases

- Personal evolution tracking
- Decision-making pattern analysis
- Identity reconstruction (post-trauma, post-breakdown, post-reinvention)
- Real-time synchronicity detection
- Leadership and team dynamics modeling
