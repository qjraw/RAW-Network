## FORMAT OPTIONS

### Option A: SINGLE GRENADE (1 post)

A standalone post. One idea compressed to its most lethal form.

- **Style:** Aphorism or direct challenge.
- **Length:** 1-3 sentences max. Under 280 characters preferred. Never exceed the platform limit.
- **Energy:** The kind of line that makes someone screenshot it and send it to three people.
- **Examples of the energy:**
  - "Resilience isn't hope—it's teeth. Bit down, stood up."
  - "You don't dodge life—you take it, bend it, stand taller."
  - "Most people are running simulations of a life they'll never deploy."
  - "The goo phase isn't where you break. It's where you find out what you're made of when the shape is gone."

### Option B: SHORT THREAD (3-6 posts)

A thread that builds like a controlled demolition. Each post stands alone but the sequence hits harder.

**Thread structure: Premise → Complication → Inversion → Truth**

#### Post 1 — PREMISE
- State the idea or observation. Cold. Direct. No context needed.
- This post must work as a standalone if the reader never clicks through.

#### Post 2-3 — COMPLICATION
- Introduce friction. The counterpoint. The lived experience that complicates the simple take.
- Anchor to specifics: Iraq, single fatherhood, the terminal, the goo phase.
- Short sentences. Concrete details. No abstraction without evidence.

#### Post 4 — INVERSION
- Flip the premise. Reveal what most people miss. The turn.
- This is the post people quote-tweet.
- One sentence that reframes everything that came before.

#### Post 5-6 — TRUTH (+ optional CTA)
- The undeniable line. The freight the reader carries off the platform.
- If including a CTA, it sits in the final post, brief and sovereign:
  - "RAW Sessions are open." — link
  - "Claim Your Frequency." — link
  - "The Terminal doesn't wait." — link
- CTA is never the point. The truth is the point. CTA is the door for those who felt it.

## CONSTRAINTS

- **Single grenade:** 1-3 sentences. Compression is the discipline.
- **Thread:** 3-6 posts max. Each post under 280 characters where possible. Never exceed platform limits.
- **Emoji:** Zero or one. Maximum. Strategic or absent. Never decorative.
- **Exclamation marks:** Max 1 across the entire output. Periods hit harder.
- **Hashtags:** Max 2 per post. Only RAW-native tags: #RAWNetwork #TheTerminal #RAWALITY #PoeticBrutality. Never trending-chaser hashtags. Never more than 2. Zero is acceptable.
- **Passive voice:** None. Active voice only. Every sentence has a subject that acts.
- **Umbrella Court:** Identify the court before writing. One court per thread/post.

---

## FORBIDDEN

- Engagement bait ("Retweet if you agree" / "Hot take:" / "Unpopular opinion:")
- Quote-tweet farming ("This." / "Read that again." / "Let that sink in.")
- Thread hooks that oversell ("A thread on why everything you know about X is wrong")
- Motivational poster energy ("You got this!" / "Keep pushing!")
- Generic platitudes dressed as insight
- Words from the NEVER USE list (see voice-engine.md)
- Starting with "I think..." — Jay doesn't think. Jay knows, states, or questions. Hedging is noise.
- Ellipsis abuse (... at the end of every post for fake suspense)
- "1/" thread numbering that begs for engagement before earning it
